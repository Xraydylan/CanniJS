Main Menu
In the main menu you have several commands, which you can use:
info/i
Display Member Info Sheet.

stats/st
Show your stats.

use points/up
Use your available status points.

inventory/inv
Open you inventory.

shop/sh
Go to the shops.

weapons/w/equipment/equip/eq
View and select your weapons.

scan/sc
Scan for Enemies and engage in battle.

help/h
View help.
