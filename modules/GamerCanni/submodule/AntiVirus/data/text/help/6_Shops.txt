Shops
Outside of battle you can go to different shops and buy new items or weapons.
Depending on your level you get to see different shops.

First use the shop/sh command to get a list of the available shops.
To select one type the number.

After a shop is selected you have to choose a category of the shop in the same way.

Now you get a list of all available item/weapons.

For information about an item/weapon use:
info/i number

To buy an item/weapon use:
buy/bu number

To you one selection menu back use:
back/b

To entirely leave the shop use:
exit/e
