Shops
Outside of battle you can visit shops to buy new items or weapons.
New shops will become available as your level increases.

Use the shop/sh command to see a list of available shops.
To visit a shop, type its number.

when visiting a shop, you must choose a shop category in the same way.
This will show you a list of all available item/weapons.

For information about an item/weapon, use:
info/i number

To buy an item/weapon, use:
buy/bu number

To go back to the previous menu, use:
back/b

To leave a shop, use:
exit/e
